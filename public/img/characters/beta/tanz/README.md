# 🦄 Tanz

Welcome to the reference folder for **Tanz**!

This character was one of my very first attempts at designing fresh characters, and I probably abandoned her because I wasn't thrilled with the design at the time. She was just sitting there, forgotten in a backup folder on my computer, so I decided to bring her here to give her a second chance at life and finally create a purpose for her existence.

## 🎨 About the Design
This character is completely original and was 100% designed and created by me.
