# Hiroshi Team VS Marlon

This track represents the moment Vinny, Hiroshi, and Rayane finally encounter Marlon for the first time in Chapter 2, serving as the soundtrack for the scene where this takes place.
Unlike my other songs, this was the second time I tried to create something more cinematic rather than just a song in itself.

The music was written inspired by "BIG SHOT" from Deltarune Chapter 2.
