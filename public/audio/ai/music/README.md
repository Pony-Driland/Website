# 🎵 AI-Generated Music: Guidelines & Terms

## About the Music
Although these tracks are AI-generated, they are far from "automatic." I personally select the instrumentation and perform extensive audio editing and adaptations to ensure the music feels human and polished reflecting my experience with music production software.

Some tracks, despite the AI assistance, are directly **derived from** or inspired by compositions I created entirely from scratch. Because of this hybrid creative process, specific rules apply to how these pieces are shared and used.

## Terms of Use
By sharing or using these tracks, you agree to the following conditions:

* **Proper Attribution:** You must inform your audience that the music was produced with AI assistance and clearly credit me as the author.
* **Transparency:** You must always redirect users to the original page where these terms of use can be read clearly.
* **No False Ownership:** You may never share these tracks as if they were your own creation.
* **Competition Rules:** You shall not enter these tracks into music competitions against other artists unless AI-generated music is explicitly permitted by the event.

## Permissions

### 🎥 Video Content
You are allowed to use these tracks in your own videos, provided that:

### 🎨 Fan-made Versions
You are permitted to create fan-made versions or remixes of these tracks, as long as you maintain proper credit according to the guidelines stated above.
