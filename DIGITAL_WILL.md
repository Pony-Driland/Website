# Legacy Statement & Posthumous Directives: Pony Driland

- My github account name: Yasmin Seidel
- My internet username: JasminDreasond

## 1. Activation Clause
These directives shall take effect upon the public confirmation of my death or a prolonged disappearance that leads to such confirmation. 

## 2. Content Continuity and Canon
* **Cessation of Official Releases:** Upon the activation of this clause, all official content releases authored by me shall cease.
* **Canon Integrity:** Should this repository be continued or maintained by another individual or entity, no new "official canon" shall be established beyond what was published during my lifetime. The existing story remains fixed as it stands at the time of my passing.

## 3. Licensing
* **License Permanence:** All existing licenses within this repository remain unchanged. This ensures that the cultural freedom and openness of the project continue indefinitely.
* **Non-Alterability:** These terms cannot be modified by any individual, organization, or entity—including the Pony Driland organization itself.
* **Fan Works and Derivative Content:** All content produced or generated by me remains open for the community. Users are free to create fan art, fan fiction, and other derivative works, provided that proper credit is attributed to the original creator.

## 4. Preservation of Essence
My primary wish is for this work to be preserved with the utmost respect. Any continuation, tribute, or study of the work must maintain the original essence of each character and the foundational structure of the story. The core identity of the world and its inhabitants must be respected regardless of the era in which the work is being engaged.

## 5. Forks and Project Maintenance
* **Preservation Forks:** I highly welcome and encourage "Preservation Forks" aimed at archiving the original, unaltered state of the project.
* **Modified Forks:** Users are also permitted to create modified forks, provided they adhere to the principles of character essence mentioned in Section 4.

## 6. Character Tributes
For those wishing to honor my memory through creative works:
* **Primary Recommendation:** The character **Yasmin** is the most recommended for tributes, as she serves as my direct representation within the story (as documented in her official wiki).
* **Secondary Option:** My main OC, **Tiny Jasmini**, may also be used for such purposes.

## 7. Repository Status (Read-Only Mode)
To respect the integrity of these directives and the preservation of my work, all official repositories on GitHub or any other platform initially created and published by me should be transitioned to **Read-Only** mode. This transition can be executed by an authorized user or by the platform's administrative staff.
