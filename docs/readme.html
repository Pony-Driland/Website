About

Pony Driland is a fanfic project involving science fiction, mystery, and drama, and some other topics that you can find out about through the website. This repository stores all files related to the canonical story of Pony Driland. The project development is being worked on in a private repository. This repository is used to share the final version of parts of the project and website.

Feel free to use this repository to talk about the project. You can check the progress of the project's development in this readme.

AI Disclaimer

All chapters of the fic were 100% human written. Due to the huge number of characters (and because I am a single person both writing, both taking care of the character designs), the designs of some characters were originally generated by AI and you will see in the credits what these images are. 80% of characters design development first involves a pure AI version and then converts it to a final version.

But I don't always have the free time to do this with all the characters. I hope you understand that I need free time to draw final versions. (and sometimes money when I give up and decide to pay an artist to make the final version.)

The use of AI for Pony Driland content is permitted as long as you are making a model that does not steal other people's private art styles. The definition of private style is defined as a style of art that was made specifically for a specific project by an private artist. Always try to use public art styles that everyone already uses.

Please also avoid using drawings from other artists without permission. There is no set date, but soon I will make Lora models myself using my own future drawings to avoid this type of problem. I will make a dedicated page just for this. The base model has already been chosen and tested. I just need time to do the drawings for the Lora models. Other artists who are doing collaborative work (fan made) are also welcome.

Inspirations about

If you are more curious about how inspirations happened, you can read exactly here a little about this subject. But remember that these texts will say a little about the Pony Driland universe. Then we recommend you read the fic before you read this text.

Undertale

The Pony Driland universe consists of characters from various universes being trapped inside the Pony Driland dimension. The story greatly explores the power of these characters' feelings, and this characteristic was very inspired by the Undertale universe. The feelings of the characters can directly interfere with the state of their souls that may have several different effects on the development of each character.

The greatest manifestation of this inspiration is linked to Rainbow Queen, but this is only related to her power. (Although Rainbow Queen has references to the character Asriel, this is just a visual reference.)

Glitchtale Season 2

The expansion of ideas on how the power of the characters' souls can be explored came directly from here. I've commented a lot on Undertale, so I don't have much to say here.

But there is another visual reference that inspired me here. While Undertale delivered to Rainbow Queen visual inspirations from Asriel, here was delivered visual inspirations from Bête Noire. That makes you better understand where I had the idea of Rainbow Queen owning a scythe. But the way the two characters use scythe has different ways. Rainbow Queen uses her scythe as much as a weapon, as to protect control of her gigantic amount of power.

Fallout Equestria

A little of the survival theme was added to Pony Driland, and some parts came directly from the Fallout game. But the most inspired part was the Pip-Boy that inspired the creation of the Smartmobies idea. The biggest difference between the two devices is that Smartmoby is directly connected to the soul and existence of the character within the Pony Driland universe.

Although there is a physical look of Smartmoby, inside the dimension of Pony Driland this device becomes invisible, just showing the screen on the character's arm when this character is using it.

Madoka Magica

More elements about soul-use powers have been inspired here. Elements of Pony Driland as the dark energy that corrupts the soul of the characters also had inspirations that emerged exactly from this anime.

The Madoka's universe helped as much as the Undertale in the development part as the connection between emotions and souls of the characters. Maybe you'll find some characters in the story that I inspired in personality of Kyubey, Madoka and, Kyoko.

Dragon Ball Super/Z

The Dragon Ball universe was a great help in the inspiration part of how the characters' powers evolve more naturally. This anime also helped me explore how very powerful characters behave, how they hide their powers, how they wish to reveal their powers, and various other personality characteristics of fighters. The fic seeks to explore the evolution of character powers in a very inspired way by Dragon Ball.

Dragon Ball Super helped more specifically in the development of divine characters from Pony Driland. This helped me a lot to quickly create an original concept of Pony Driland divinity. Just like Undertale and Glitchtale did, Dragon Ball Super left an little visual easter egg in Rainbow Queen inspired by the character Beerus.

I also had power inspirations in characters like Cell and Majin Buu, but I will let you yourselves find where that is.

AI Tips for writers

Understand that these tips are not for AI to write a fic for you, but to help you yourself be more productive when writing your fic. First you need an AI environment that accepts to interpret your entire story, regardless of its size.
If your fic is on a public website and it's free, it won't make any difference you use a corporate AI or a private AI environment locally on your computer. Because your fic may already have been used to train AI by these companies or physical people who create AI models to write roleplay or anything else.

The easiest way to deal with this is to accept this reality so you feel safer to continue with the process. After choosing where you want to practice the tips below, you can continue seeing the tips now.

Avoid emulations of your fic through AI

The less developed your fic, the more artificial it will be when AI tries to emulate your characters and the fic universe. I don't like doing this kind of thing because AI will always try to mix your story and what AI model thinks "your characters should be". First of all, AI is just an emulation about what AI already knows what exists, that means that AI will not try to create a new idea, but reuse what already exists to try to create something new.

If your fic does not yet have an identity, it will destroy the creative development of your story. You will always be stuck to what has already been developed in your fic and what the AI model knows. Although I don’t like this idea, I can say that maybe this works better if your fic is too advanced in the development enough to emulate the entire universe of your fic. A fic completely rich in itself identity can be much easier to use AI for this kind of thing.

You can try to use AI to try to have an idea about the decisions of your characters, but making a complete emulation is not recommended. Leave this part exclusively for your community's fan service that will probably use this to create roleplay chatbots using your fic characters. My opinion is that emulation of characters is fun if you are doing this for entertainment, and not for development.

Request text fixes

I just started using AI for this kind of thing in the beginning of 2024, so this correction process can still take a long time depending on my patience. (saying this in 10/30/2024) Ask AI several times where there are possible writing issues in your fic, this can be extremely useful if your English is not yet fully fluent. If your native language is not English and you ask this question through your language, be careful, sometimes AI can try to translate the name of your characters to your native language.

Your best friend to talk about your Fic

One of the more things that can be common for writers is the loneliness of being able to talk to someone about their fic. Sometimes you have many friends who have no patience to stay reading everything you wrote to ask for feedback.

This may be the most extremely interesting part of you using your AI. You can have a time of your day and put your fic inside an AI to stay several hours chatting about everything that happened in the story. Imagine you can have a friend who you can talk about your fic at the time you want, when you want, about anything you want, this is the experience of using AI, making all this experience very fun. But this will depend solely on the writer's creativity in asking questions for AI, because AI will only provide you a good conversation if you starts these good conversations.

You can ask about what AI thinks about the last parts you've posted from your fic, question what AI thinks about your characters and explore what your readers might be thinking when reading your story right now.

This part is interesting, but I would recommend you to do on a locally closed AI on your computer. You can ask AI about theories that AI thinks about your fic. Sometimes the answers to these questions can help you bring inspiration to make these theories something canonical or feed conspiracy theories that can be developed inside your fic to leave your reader even more stuck in wanting to understand what is happening inside your story.

This allows you yourself to experience a very deep immersion about your own universe of your fic. The more developed the universe of your fic, the easier and natural it will be the AI helping you in this kind of conversation.
